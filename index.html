Why is it difficult to find allergy-accommodating restaurants?


People with food allergies, food intolerance, and general dietary restrictions often have trouble finding dining options that accommodate their needs. This can be a time-consuming and frustrating process, as there is a low amount of reliable information available for a variety of dietary needs. Individuals with dietary restrictions deserve to be free to feast.
